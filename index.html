Huaijin Wang

The calculation of classical polynomials and its Gaussian integral.

Computing classic orthogonal polynomials and Gauss quadratures.

  • lepoly.m compute Legendre polynomials and its first-order derivatives.
  • legs.m compute Legendre-Gauss quadrature nodes and weights.
  • Jacobi_Nodes.m compute Jacobi polynomials and its first-order derivatives.
  • jags.m compute Jacobi-Gauss quadrature nodes and weights.
  • lapoly.m compute Laguerre polynomials and their first-order derivatives.
  • lags.m compute Laguerre-Gauss quadrature nodes and weights.

Computing Müntz-Legendre polynomials.

  • legendre.m compute Müntz Legendre polynomials.
  • search.m find the optimal sigma in computing Müntz Legendre polynomials.
  • muntz_legendre.m evaluate Müntz Legendre polynomials and their first-order derivatives for all lambda.
  • muntz_legendre3.m is the same as muntz_legendre.m , but accelerated by dynamic programming.

Computing Müntz-Gauss quadratures.

  • continuation_weighted.m compute the Müntz-Gauss quadrature weighted by power function.
  • gaussQuad_weighted.m implement Newton iteration for each step in continuation.
  • generateC.m compute moments of the Müntz Legendre polynomials with respect to a power function.
  • generatesUXYZ.m compute the Jacobi Matrix in Newton iteration.
  • moments_recursive.m compute the moments of Müntz Legendre polynomials with respect to a class of singular functions.
  • my_chebyshev.m compute the recurrence coefficients for a class of orthogonal polynomials weighted by a singular function.
  • singular_gauss_quadrature.m compute the Gauss quadrature weighted by a singular function.
